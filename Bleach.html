Bleach is a Japanese Animation in the supernatural and adventure genre, in which select humans are given the rights to become a Shinigami(otherwise known as the japanese grim reaper), and they are called "Soul Reapers". In a bizzare twist of events the main protagonist Ichigo Kurosaki(Itch-E-Go Koo-Roh-Saki), already has the ability to see the spirits of the dead, encounters a Soul Reaper name Rukia Kuchiki(Roo-Key-uh Koo-Chi-key), on one of her missions to kill an evil spirit, and steals her Soul Reaper powers on accident. This sparks conflict after conflict that makes Ichigo and Rukia grow more and more to care for the people around them.
