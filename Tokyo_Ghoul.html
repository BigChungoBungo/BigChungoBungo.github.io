Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese Animation based on the mythos of vampires, while giving it a very grusome twist. Due to some genetic accident in ancient Tokyo, humans began to eat other humans regularly. This sprouted the term ghouls, this problem was easily avoidable, until these "ghouls" developed genetic weapons called "Kagunes"(Kah-Goon-Ays) which the used to fight of humans attempting to exterminate them. A task force name the CCG in present day Tokyo was established, and using ghouls whom were killed kagunes against other ghouls in order to exterminate them for good. This story centers around Ken Kaneki, a boy who suffered a freak accident, and got his organs tranfered with that of a ghouls, in turn making him become one. It has a beautiful action-packed story that offers much to think about.
