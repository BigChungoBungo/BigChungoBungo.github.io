My Hero Academia(Boku No Hero Academia) is a japanese animation based around an idea in which, around 80% of the human population has gained special physical and mental properties called quirks that give them super powers, such as flight, or shooting fire out of their hands. The show circles around a school for these individuals that trains them to become heroes and to help the normal populace. Izuku Midoriya is the main character, and the story surrounds him overcoming bullying from an old rival, conflict within himself, and his self-doubt.
